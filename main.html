Summary

Inception (2010) is a science-fiction heist film directed by Christopher Nolan. The story follows Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who specializes in extracting secrets from people's subconscious during dream states. Cobb is offered a chance to have his criminal record erased if he can successfully perform an almost-impossible task: plant an idea into someone's mind (inception) rather than steal one. The film explores themes of memory, grief, reality vs. illusion, and the ethics of manipulating minds.

Actors

Main cast and roles in Inception:

  • Leonardo DiCaprio as Dom Cobb (the extractor and protagonist)
  • Joseph Gordon-Levitt as Arthur (Cobb's point-man)
  • Ellen Page as Ariadne (the architect)
  • Tom Hardy as Eames (the forger)
  • Ken Watanabe as Saito (the client)
  • Marion Cotillard as Mal (Cobb's late wife)

Each actor contributes strongly to the film’s blend of emotional stakes and high-concept action.
